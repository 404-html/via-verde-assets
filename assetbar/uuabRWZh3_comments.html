The Old Fashioned Geek Chinaman N-Bomb

2oz whiskey
1/2oz dry vermouth
1/2oz Pernod
Dash Angostura Bitters
1 huamei (preserved plum)
Soda

Fill a tumbler with ice and top with a huamei or two. Add the whiskey, vermouth, Pernod, and bitters, and stir. Top with soda. Let it sit for a minute so the huamei can "steep" before drinking.

(Huamei are crazy little things that you can find at an Asian market, I guess in like the snack food section. The only English I can find on my bag says "PRESERVED FRUITS" so identifying them may be nontrivial. Alternately, you could substitute with a maraschino cherry, making an Old Fashioned Geek Croat N-Bomb.)
